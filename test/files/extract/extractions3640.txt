9 MONTREAL — A Montreal man has died after an incident with city police this morning. Provincial police say Montreal officers were responding to a psychological distress call at an apartment block at about 8 a.m. They are not confirming multiple media reports the man was shot. He was taken to hospital and was declared dead several hours later. The incident occurred about three blocks south of the Olympic Stadium in the city”s Hochelaga-Maisonneuve borough. Provincial police have taken over the case. In Quebec, it is customary for another force to investigate when there is injury or death during a police operation.

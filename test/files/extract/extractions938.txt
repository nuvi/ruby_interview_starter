Ronaldo to play against Manchester City as Zidane insists Real Madrid star is &apos;fine&apos; 
Cristiano Ronaldo is set to start against Manchester City on Tuesday after Real Madrid coach Zinedine Zidane declared the Portuguese forward 100 percent fit. Karim Benzema is also expected to be in the starting XI for the Champions League semi final first leg at the Etihad on Tuesday after recovering from a thigh injury. Real will make final checks on the pair over the next 24 hours, but the news is a huge boost to the Spanish champions as they go in search of a record 11th European Cup.

Rendezvous solves the challenges of running a busy meeting venue Posted April 25, 2016 by Heather Hook 
Rendezvous Events Venue Management Software is the venue booking software that provides you with complete control over resources and rooms, with all the tools you need to manage payments, oversee tasks, reserve space and track booking alterations in real-time. 
Be it from boosting sales to diary management or streamlining your booking process, Rendezvous from NFS is for you. 
With Rendezvous you will have the tools to… 
&gt; Generate more revenue by increasing conversions&gt; Streamline event operations&gt; Increase revenue from existing clients&gt; Create exciting event layout diagrams&gt; Handle complex event billing&gt; Integrate with AV and digital media&gt; Generate management Information like sales forecasts. 
Winnie Simelane, Senior Events Coordinator at the Innovation Hub, Comments: 
“We implemented Rendezvous to improve the management of our meeting venues. It has helped us streamline our operation and release more time to develop new business and look after our customers better. 
The integration across enquiry handling, diary management, operations and billing is an important reason why Rendezvous is a great tool for the Innovation Hub”.

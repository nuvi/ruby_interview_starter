Posted Apr. 24, 2016 at 12:22 PM Posted Apr. 24, 2016 at 12:22 PM NORTH ANDOVER HAT: National Public Gardens Day WHEN: Friday, May 6, 10 a.m. to 2 p.m. WHERE: Stevens-Coolidge Place, 137 Andover St., North Andover COST: $5 members, $10 non-members. Full House tour an additional $5 for non-members, free for members. INFO: 978-682-3580; www.thetrustees.org/places-to-visit/north-shore/stevens-coolidge-place.html Garden tours, lawn games, house tours of the historic Stevens-Coolidge Place country estate will be part of National Public Gardens Day at the Stevens-Coolidge Place. Explore a simple circa 1800 farmhouse that was transformed into an elegant gentleman’s estate. Step back to the 1920s as you are guided through the home with its collection of Chinese porcelain and artifacts from the Coolidge’s travels through Asia and Europe in the early 1900s. Lawn games will also be set up from 10 a.m. to 2 p.m. No preregistration is necessary. Bring a picnic and stay all day and or come for only part of the day. Parking is available across Andover Street on Chestnut Street. North of Boston

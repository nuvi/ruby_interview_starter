Aleris to Announce First-Quarter 2016 Financial Results on May 5 14:53 ET from Aleris CLEVELAND , April 25, 2016 /PRNewswire/ -- Aleris is scheduled to announce first-quarter 2016 financial results on Thursday, May 5, 2016 . Logo - http://photos.prnewswire.com/prnh/20140115/CL46886LOGO President and Chief Executive Officer Sean Stack and Chief Financial Officer and Treasurer Eric Rychel will host an earnings conference call to discuss the company&apos;s first-quarter financial performance and take questions from analysts at 9 a.m. ET on May 5 , 2016. Conference Call and Slide Presentation Live dial-in number: 1-866-364-3817 or 1-412-902-4207 for international callers Please ask for the Aleris Call (Please dial in 10 minutes before conference call start time) Live webcast: https://www.webcaster4.com/Webcast/Page/1082/14886 Analysts who wish to ask a question following the prepared remarks should press &quot;star and 1&quot; on their push button phone during the call. Your name will be placed in queue. If you need assistance, press &quot;star zero&quot; on your touch tone phone to reach the conference operator. A replay of the webcast will be available in the Investor Relations section of the company&apos;s website. About Aleris Aleris is a privately held, global leader in aluminum rolled products serving diverse industries including aerospace, automotive, building and construction, commercial transportation and industrial manufacturing. Headquartered in Cleveland, Ohio , Aleris operates production facilities in North America , Europe and Asia . For more information, visit www.aleris.com .

Amazon.com, Inc. (NASDAQ:AMZN) ‘s stock had its “buy” rating reiterated by Cantor Fitzgerald in a note issued to investors on Monday, ARN reports. 
Shares of Amazon.com ( NASDAQ:AMZN ) traded up 0.757% during midday trading on Monday, hitting $625.195. 1,779,718 shares of the company were exchanged. The stock has a market cap of $294.37 billion and a PE ratio of 500.156. Amazon.com has a one year low of $414.55 and a one year high of $696.44. The stock’s 50-day moving average is $589.42 and its 200-day moving average is $602.52. 
Amazon.com (NASDAQ:AMZN) last issued its earnings results on Thursday, January 28th. The e-commerce giant reported $1.00 earnings per share for the quarter, missing the Zacks’ consensus estimate of $1.56 by $0.56. The company had revenue of $35.75 billion for the quarter, compared to analyst estimates of $35.99 billion. During the same quarter last year, the firm posted $0.45 EPS. The company’s revenue was up 21.9% compared to the same quarter last year. Equities research analysts anticipate that Amazon.com will post $4.79 EPS for the current year. 
Several institutional investors recently made changes to their positions in the stock. Norges Bank bought a new position in shares of Amazon.com during the fourth quarter worth $2,089,228,000. Vanguard Group Inc. raised its position in shares of Amazon.com by 2.9% in the fourth quarter. Vanguard Group Inc. now owns 22,010,802 shares of the e-commerce giant’s stock worth $14,876,881,000 after buying an additional 612,597 shares during the period. Sands Capital Management LLC raised its position in shares of Amazon.com by 21.1% in the fourth quarter. Sands Capital Management LLC now owns 2,113,048 shares of the e-commerce giant’s stock worth $1,428,188,000 after buying an additional 367,494 shares during the period. Lone Pine Capital LLC raised its position in shares of Amazon.com by 14.0% in the fourth quarter. Lone Pine Capital LLC now owns 2,207,252 shares of the e-commerce giant’s stock worth $1,491,860,000 after buying an additional 270,250 shares during the period. Finally, Massachusetts Financial Services Co. MA raised its position in shares of Amazon.com by 17.9% in the fourth quarter. Massachusetts Financial Services Co. MA now owns 1,771,171 shares of the e-commerce giant’s stock worth $1,197,116,000 after buying an additional 268,651 shares during the period. 
Other equities analysts have also recently issued reports about the stock. JMP Securities reduced their price objective on shares of Amazon.com from $740.00 to $710.00 and set a “market outperform” rating for the company in a research report on Friday, January 29th. Robert W. Baird reaffirmed an “outperform” rating and set a $710.00 price objective on shares of Amazon.com in a research report on Wednesday, January 20th. Vetr raised shares of Amazon.com from a “buy” rating to a “strong-buy” rating and set a $707.29 price objective for the company in a research report on Monday, January 11th. Wedbush reaffirmed an “outperform” rating and set a $700.00 price objective on shares of Amazon.com in a research report on Tuesday, April 19th. Finally, Bank of America reaffirmed a “buy” rating and set a $750.00 price objective on shares of Amazon.com in a research report on Monday, April 11th. Seven analysts have rated the stock with a hold rating and forty have given a buy rating to the company. Amazon.com presently has a consensus rating of “Buy” and a consensus target price of $724.91. 
In other Amazon.com news, Director Patricia Q. Stonesifer sold 6,250 shares of Amazon.com stock in a transaction on Thursday, March 3rd. The shares were sold at an average price of $577.94, for a total value of $3,612,125.00. Following the transaction, the director now owns 19,173 shares in the company, valued at approximately $11,080,843.62. The sale was disclosed in a document filed with the SEC, which can be accessed through the SEC website . Also, Director Thomas O. Ryder sold 4,000 shares of Amazon.com stock in a transaction on Tuesday, February 16th. The shares were sold at an average price of $516.93, for a total transaction of $2,067,720.00. Following the completion of the transaction, the director now owns 20,653 shares in the company, valued at approximately $10,676,155.29. The disclosure for this sale can be found here .
